Given 2 integer arrays X and Y of same size. Consider both arrays as vectors and print the sum of maximum scalar product (Dot product) of 2 vectors.

Sample input 1:

4

1 2 3 4

5 6 7 8

Sample output 1:

70

Explanation :

(8*4 + 7*3 + 6*2 + 1*5) = 70

Sample input 2:

4

-1 -2 -3 -4

5 6 -7 -8

Sample output 2:

36

Explanation :

(-4*-8 + -3*-7 + -2*6 + -1*5) = 36
